Lung Nodules Detector

Introducing a machine learning (ML) model designed to detect tumors in CT scans! This model is trained to analyze CT images and identify abnormal growths with high accuracy, helping radiologists and doctors spot potential tumors earlier.

The model works by learning patterns from thousands of labeled CT scans, distinguishing healthy tissue from possible tumor areas. With advanced image-processing techniques, it can highlight regions of interest, making it a valuable tool in diagnostic imaging.
